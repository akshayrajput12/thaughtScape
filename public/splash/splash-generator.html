CampusCash PWA Splash Screen Generator

Instructions:

  1. Enter an Unsplash image URL for the background
  2. Optionally add a logo image URL
  3. Enter your app name and tagline
  4. Adjust colors and opacity
  5. Click "Generate Splash Screens" to preview
  6. Click "Download All Splash Screens" to save the files
  7. Place the downloaded files in your project's /public/splash/ directory
